A Little Scratching

After admiring the Bridge of Sighs, walk past the Ponte della Paglia and take the first road on the left.

This is the calle degli Albanesi, which runs along the ancient jailhouse.

Pay attention to the large stones, and you will notice that one of them is particularly worn. Why?

When the guards were on patrol, they had to wear very heavy armor. Often, they grew tired and leaned against the stone to rest.

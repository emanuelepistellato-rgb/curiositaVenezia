Stargate

Over the centuries, in the narrow space of the portico of the Church of San Simeon Grando, inexplicable disappearances of people occurred. [cite: 2]

In the portico, there is the shrine of San Ermolao, a white marble tombstone sculpted in 1382. [cite: 3] The saint is depicted standing, wrapped in a cloak, with hands open to reveal his palms. [cite: 3]

A theory suggests that the bas-relief, whose shape resembles a door, hides a passage to other universes. [cite: 5]

The belief is that it is enough to place your fingertips on the saint’s fingers, make a wish to leave, and press gently. [cite: 6] The entire marble stele would sink into the wall, absorbing the human presence. [cite: 7]

For it to work, however, the portal required a conductor of energy: a large wooden cross. [cite: 8] This cross was removed in the 1960s and has been missing ever since. [cite: 9]

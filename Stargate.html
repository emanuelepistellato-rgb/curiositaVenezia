Stargate

Over the centuries, in the narrow space of the portico of the Church of San Simeon Grando, inexplicable disappearances of people occurred.

In this portico, there is the shrine of San Ermolao, a white marble tombstone sculpted in 1382.

A theory suggests that the bas-relief, whose shape resembles a door, hides a passage to other universes.

The saint is depicted standing with his arms raised and hands open. It is believed that placing your fingertips on the saint's fingers and pressing gently could cause the entire stele to sink into the wall, absorbing the human presence.

For it to work, the portal required a conductor of energy: a large wooden cross that was removed in the 1960s and has been missing ever since.
